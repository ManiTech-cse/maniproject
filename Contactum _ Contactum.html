About Contactum

 

Contactum has been a proud British Manufacturer of electrical accessories and circuit protection since 1936. At Contactum, we are focused on quality, reliability, functionality and our commitment to manufacturing in the UK.

 

Contactum, began life in 1936 and we still manufacture our comprehensive range of wiring accessories products at our factory in Cricklewood London and UK Head Office based in Dunstable, Bedfordshire.

Circuit Protection

 

Contactum has an extensive range of circuit protection equipment including a comprehensive range of Defender 1.0 & 2.0 Consumer Units type tested to BS EN 61439-3 including annex ZB Surge protection consumer units and devices, tested to relevent standards, New B Board and IP66 Steel Enclosures.
